Lipstick
It comes in various forms, including traditional bullet lipsticks, liquid lipsticks, lip glosses, and lip balms, offering different finishes and levels of pigmentation. Lipstick shades range from subtle nudes and soft pinks to bold reds and deep berries, allowing for endless possibilities to complement different skin tones and preferences. Lipstick formulations may include hydrating ingredients like oils and butters to keep the lips smooth and comfortable throughout wear.
Matte lipstick offers a velvety, non-shiny finish with rich, saturated color payoff. Its long-wearing formula provides a bold, statement lip that stays put throughout the day without smudging or transferring.
Sheer lipstick delivers a subtle hint of color with a lightweight, translucent finish. It adds a natural-looking tint to the lips, providing moisture and shine for a fresh, effortless look.
