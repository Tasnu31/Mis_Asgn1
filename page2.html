Blush
It typically comes in powder, cream, or liquid form, allowing for various application techniques and finishes. Blush shades range from soft pinks and peaches to vibrant corals and bronzes, providing options to enhance different skin tones and achieve desired looks. Blush can be applied to the apples of the cheeks and blended upward toward the temples to create a youthful, radiant complexion. It adds dimension to the face, brightening the complexion and accentuating facial features.
Powder blush comes in a pressed or loose powder form, offering buildable coverage and a matte or satin finish. It blends seamlessly into the skin, providing a soft, natural flush that can be layered for intensity.
Cream blush has a creamy texture that melts into the skin, delivering a dewy, luminous finish. It provides a sheer wash of color that can be easily blended with fingers or a brush for a radiant, lit-from-within glow.
